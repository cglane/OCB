The Original Charleston Bedswing® is all about relaxation. Porches, especially in the South, are the preferred venue for enjoying a cold glass of sweet tea, long talks with loved ones, reading a good book, and lazy afternoon naps. They act as the intermediary between the outside world and the intimate life of the home.

Wanting to make our porch more inviting and comfortable for friends and family, I built the first Bedswing. In the beginning I made each bed swing by hand, giving them to friends and loved ones as gifts. As word spread and demand grew, it became clear that I would need to hand over production to someone else. Producing the swings anywhere but locally was not an option.

After enlisting several local wood shops with varying success, I met Pat Withers and knew I did not need to search any further. I work closely with Pat and his employees at Withers Industries to ensure each bed swing is of the same quality as the first one I built so many years ago. The quality is unbeatable.

Lean back in one and it becomes clear that every porch deserves an Original Charleston Bedswing®.
